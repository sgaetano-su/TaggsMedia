Business / Marketing

You work hard to make your business successful . . . so we do too.

The Process

Pre-Production

An experienced producer will sit down with someone from your business in order to figure out your goals, audience, and strategize ideas and concepts that will help tell the story of your initiative the best.

Production

We’ll put your vision into action by shooting a dynamic range of footage and interviews to use in the post-production phase of the process.

Post-Production

Now, it’s time to edit. Whether it’s a full length promo or a short piece of social media content, our editor will work hard to polish everything we’ve collected so far in one beautiful final film.

Services

To best serve your vision for your business, we allow you to mix and match any of the following services, that way, you can get quality content that fits into your budget.

Promotional Video

A longer, documentary type, branding video perfect for featuring on the front page of your website or showing new clients/investors.

Commercial

A short, 1 minute or less video that can be used as a TV. spot, on the front page of your website, or another place on the internet.

Real Estate

We can provide stunning photography & videography for properties ranging from a single home, to a set of apartments, and even commercial spaces.

Social Media Content

Brief videos edited individually for specific use on social media platforms as paid or unpaid advertisement.

Event / Experience Recap

Event/experience recaps take what you feel during an experience and translate them to the digital screen. We provide highlight-based video for a variety of experiences including concerts, festivals, and summer camps.
