Weddings

We provide story-driven packages in a variety of price ranges for all of the following family events:

Services

To best serve your vision for your business, we allow you to mix and match any of the following services, that way, you can get quality content that fits into your budget.

Feature Film

The Feature Film tells the story of your event from the start of preparation, to the arrival on location, to toast and speeches, to the very finish of festivities…

Includes:
• In-person Planning Session
• 7-10 minute of story driven film
• 1080p quality

Optional Add-ons:
• 1 minute teaser to release immediately after the event leading up to the release of the full feature
• 4-5 minute same-day video montage

Highlight Video

The Highlights Video captures the moments from your event that you’ll cherish the most (does not include pre-event footage) and brings them together in one special video.

Includes:
• In-person Planning Session
• 4-6 minutes of story driven film
• 1080p quality

Optional Add-ons:
• 1 minute teaser to release immediately after the event leading up to the release of the full feature
• 4-5 minute same-day video montage

Photography

We’ll be at your event from start to finish shooting high definition photos…

Includes:
• In-person Planning Session
• Retouched Album
• Untouched Catalog

Optional Add-ons:
• Photo Booth
• Same-day Slideshow
• Pre-event pictures (bride and groom getting ready, limo ride, etc…)

Longform Movie

The Longform Movie provides all the moments from your special day unedited and without an overlay of music. All of the useable (not blurry or shaky) footage that we have will be compiled into one long video for you to relive your day just as you did when it happened.

Includes:
• 1 compilation video (length depends on event)
• 1080p quality

Optional Add-ons:
• 1 minute teaser to release immediately after the event leading up to the release of the full video
• 4-5 minute same-day video montage

Speeches / Ceremonies

We’ll capture a high quality video and audio recording of the full ceremony and/or toasts and speeches of your event.

Includes:
• The duration of the ceremony and all toasts/speeches
• 1080p quality

Event Website

Want all your pictures, videos, and recap details in one central location? We'll make a personalized website just for your event including your choice of the services above!

Includes:
• Interactive website in the theme of your event
• 1 year of a domain of your choice on us!
• 3 months of hosting on us!
